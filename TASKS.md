Operator never has to intervene; the daemon closes its own loop. Failure summary: <log excerpt>". (c) The daemon's claude pushes the fix commit; CI re-runs; next iteration checks again; loop until green or 3 retries. (d) After 3 retries, the daemon labels the PR `Blocked: daemon-stuck` and synthesises a new TASKS.md entry summarising what's blocked (operator-actionable). (e) Composes with `daemon-pre-pr-lint-gate` (above) ŌĆö that gate cuts most failures at PR-open time; this gate handles the residue.
